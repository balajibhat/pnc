Rooted in Place

PNC is a UAE-born brand. Its palette isn't borrowed from European minimalism or American corporate convention. Each color is a direct reference to the physical landscape where Prameela built her career and where her clients operate. The palette tells a story of place before it tells a story of profession.

03 / Primary Mark: The Three Rings

The Three Rings

Three overlapping circles form a Venn structure. Each ring carries a dual meaning: P is both Prameela and Performance. N is both Nair and Network. C is both Collective and Creative. Prameela sits at the triple intersection where all three converge. The ring colors map to the UAE palette: terracotta (earth), gulf blue (water), sage (growth).

04 / Alternate Mark: The Orbital Ring

The Orbital Ring

Three smooth arcs in terracotta, gulf blue, and sage each sweep one-third of the outer circle, meeting at accent dots that mark the transition points. Three disciplines orbiting a central point: the PNC monogram within a fine inner circle. A system in motion, not static. Three forces, three colors, one continuous path.

07 / Design Rationale

Why This Direction

This direction centers the word that makes the brand name work: Collective. The palette is drawn directly from the UAE landscape: sand dunes, terracotta heritage, green oases, the Arabian Gulf, and the gunmetal skyline at dusk. These are not borrowed colors. They belong to the place where Prameela built her career and where her clients operate.

Replacing black with gunmetal softens the palette without losing authority. The addition of gulf blue brings depth and a connection to water, commerce, and the coastal identity of the Emirates. Together, the five primary colors tell a story of place that resonates with any UAE-based client who walks into the room.

"Nobody wins alone. An end goal has to be understood and cascaded. If not, it fails."

The Cormorant Garamond typeface brings editorial elegance that suits a woman of her stature, while Outfit's clean geometry keeps everything modern and readable. The pairing says: experienced enough to appreciate the classics, sharp enough to stay current.

The PNC framework gives the brand name strategic depth. P, N, C are not just initials. They are the three pillars of the offering: Performance (data-driven growth), Network (the trusted collective model), and Creative (brand and storytelling). The Venn diagram makes this tangible. Every client engagement sits at the intersection of these three disciplines, and Prameela is the center point who holds the whole together.
